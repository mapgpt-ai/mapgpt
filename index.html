About mapgpt.ai

mapgpt.ai is an innovative AI and GIS-based application designed to bridge the gap between natural language and geospatial data. Our platform empowers users to interact with complex GIS data using simple, natural language queries.

Founded by a team of GIS experts and AI specialists, we're committed to making spatial data analysis more accessible and intuitive for everyone, from GIS professionals to business analysts.
